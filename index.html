About Women's Day

International Women's Day celebrates the social, economic, cultural, and political achievements of women. The day also marks a call to action for accelerating gender equality.

Each year on March 8, we come together to celebrate women's achievements, raise awareness about discrimination, and take action to drive gender parity. The theme for 2025 focuses on empowering women in leadership and challenging gender stereotypes.

This global day recognizes the remarkable role that women play in their communities, families, and workplaces. Join us in honoring the incredible women who have shaped our world and continue to inspire future generations.
